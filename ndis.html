What is the NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) gives people with a disability greater choice over the supports they receive. The NDIS funds services that enable people to achieve their goals, increase independence, develop skills for everyday life and participate in the community.

The NDIS is designed around you and your individual needs. It provides funding for supports and services that will help you live an ordinary life, pursue your goals, and build your capacity to participate in community and economic life.

Who We Are?

Latitude Quality Care is an NDIS provider working with people with disability to make it easier for them to access services they need under the NDIS support service. Latitude Quality Care aims to create an environment of service provision which creates a smoother journey for all involved, including our staff and participants.

We will also engage with other NDIS providers in order to share, learn and collaborate on opportunities that enable us to strengthen the support base for people who need our services.

Is the NDIS for You?

Check if you meet the eligibility criteria for NDIS support services.

Eligibility Checklist

  • Are you a person living with a disability?
  • Are you under 65 years of age?
  • Are you a parent of a child with a developmental delay?
  • Are you a permanent resident of Australia?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, you may be eligible for NDIS support.
