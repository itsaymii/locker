About Us

At Latitude Quality Care, we're more than just an NDIS provider — we're a team dedicated to helping people with disability live with greater independence, dignity, and confidence.

We work closely with participants, families, and communities to make it easier to access the right supports under the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). Our goal is simple: to create a smoother, more empowering journey for everyone involved.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, person-centred support that enhances the wellbeing, independence, and inclusion of people with disability across our community.

We believe every individual deserves the opportunity to live life on their own terms, and we’re here to make that possible through genuine care, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

What is the NDIS?

We take a collaborative approach to care — working hand-in-hand with healthcare professionals, local councils, community groups, and other NDIS providers to strengthen the support network for our participants.
