Our Services

Our team provides a wide range of NDIS support services to help you live independently and confidently. Each service is flexible and tailored to suit your goals and lifestyle.

Accommodation / Tenancy

Find a safe and comfortable place to call home. We help you secure suitable housing, understand tenancy agreements, and maintain your independence.

Group / Centre Activities

Connect, socialise and grow in inclusive group or centre settings to build skills and friendships.

Participate Community

Assistance to join community, social and recreational activities to stay active and engaged.

Household Tasks

Support with cleaning, laundry and meal prep to maintain a healthy living environment.

Development / Life Skills

Training for daily living skills like budgeting, cooking and personal care.

Assist Travel / Transport

Transport support to appointments, work or community activities.

Assist Personal Activities

Personal care tailored to individual needs (showering, dressing, grooming).

Daily Tasks / Shared Living

Support with daily tasks and shared living to build independence.

Innov Community Participation

Programs to increase community participation and skill development.

Support Coordination

Guidance to understand your NDIS plan and coordinate supports effectively.
