Understanding the NDIS

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) is an Australian Government initiative that provides funding to people with disability, giving them greater choice, control, and independence over the supports they receive.

What is the NDIS?

Through the NDIS, participants can access services that help them live life their way. The NDIS is designed to ensure people with disability get the right support, at the right time.

Is the NDIS for You?

You may be eligible for NDIS funding if you meet the following criteria:

You are living with a permanent or significant disability

You are under 65 years of age

You are an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or hold a Protected Special Category visa

You need support to complete daily activities or participate in the community

If you’re unsure about your eligibility, our friendly team can guide you through the process and help you understand your options.
